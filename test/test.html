1. Obtain the application form

From your workplace, university, or railway station.

2. Fill out the online application form

Provided on the official website.

3. Upload a photo or PDF of the completed application form

Ensure that the size of the uploaded file (photo or PDF) is less than 5MB.

4. Submit the application form

Wait for review by the station master.

5. Application review

The station master will review the application for validity.

6. Approval or rejection

Receive notification about the outcome of the application.

7. Make the payment

If approved, proceed to make the payment for the season ticket.

8. Receive the season ticket

Upon successful payment, you will receive the season ticket via email.
