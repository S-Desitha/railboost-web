instructions


Online Registration:

  • Visit the official website and log in or create an account if you haven't already.
  • Navigate to the parcel booking section and fill out the required details for your parcel.

    • Drop-off at the Station:

  • Once the online registration is complete, bring your parcel to the designated station.
  • Hand over your parcel to the station staff and inform them that you've completed the online registration.

    • Weight Measurement and Package Check:

  • The station master will measure the weight of your parcel using the appropriate equipment.
  • The station staff will conduct a thorough check of the packaging to ensure it meets the required
    standards and is suitable for transportation.

    • Attachment of Details Form:

  • After measuring and checking, the station master will attach a details form to your parcel.
  • This form will include essential information such as the tracking number, sender's details,
    recipient's details, and any special handling instructions.

    • Email Notification to Receiver:

  • Once the details form is attached, an automated email notification will be sent to the recipient's email address.
  • The email will contain the tracking number and a link to track the parcel's current location and status.

    • Parcel Tracking:

  • You can track your parcel in real-time by clicking on the provided tracking link in the email
    or by logging into your account on the website.
  • Regularly check the tracking information to stay updated on your parcel's journey and expected delivery date.
